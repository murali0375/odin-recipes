My Special Dosa

Idly is a famous South Indian food. People eat Idly for their breakfast.

Ingredients

  1. Dosa Rice 1 Kg
  2. Salt 10 gram
  3. Backing Soda 10 gram
  4. For Egg dosa - 2 eggs

Cooking Steps

  1. Soak Dosa Rice for 4 Hrs or Use Rice batter
  2. Gring the Rice
  3. move to vessal
  4. Add Backing Soda
  5. Add salt
