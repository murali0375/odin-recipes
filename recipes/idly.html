Idly

My Style Idly

Idly is a famous South Indian food. People eat Idly for their breakfast.

Ingredients

  1. Indly Batter
  2. Coconut 1 no
  3. Salt as per requirements

Cooking Steps

  1. Keep the Idly Maker on the Stove
  2. Boil the Water
  3. Fill the Idly cups with batter
  4. Insert the Idly cup in the idly maker
  5. Cook for 20 mins

Your Idly will be ready, open the Idly maker and serve hot Idlys
